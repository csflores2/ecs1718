Education:

The First School that I attended was Cardinal Bernerdin Early Childhood Center (CBECC). CBECC is a Catholic Montessori School offering Preschool through Third Grade. I attended CBECC when I was 4 years old and graduated when I was 9. This school was an amazing experience because I was able to grow up in an environment where everyone worked and collaborated with each other. The teachers and mentors taught me how to work well with other people and most importantly how to be grateful and appreciate everything I have in my life.
