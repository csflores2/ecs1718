The Giant Panda's native habitat is China. Pandas can be found in zoos and in reserves in China but only a small number have been seen in Europe or North America. A Panda's daily diet is almost entirely leaves, stems and shoots of bamboo. Bamboo contains bery little nutrition, therefore, pandas must eat aproximately 12-38kg every day to meet their energy needs.
